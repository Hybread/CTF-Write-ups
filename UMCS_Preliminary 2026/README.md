# UMCS Preliminary Round 2026

## Introduction
UMCS is an annual CTF competition hosted by the Cybersecurity club of University Malaya. This year, it featured a 24-hour online preliminary round, where over 100 teams battled it out against each other to qualify for the 
onsite A&D (Attack & Defense) finals. This year, I was fortunate enough to be able to participate and qualify for the finals with my clubmates of APU FSEC-SS alongside me. As usual, I primarily focused on Reverse Engineering and trying my best on 
Binary Exploitation, whilst my teammates solved the other categories. Anyways, please enjoy the writeups that I have prepared!
