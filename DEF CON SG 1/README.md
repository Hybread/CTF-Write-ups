DEF CON SG 1 was the very first DEF CON hacking conference that was held in South East Asia in 2026 at Marina Bay Sands, Singapore. I was fortunate enough to be able to attend this amazing event alongside 7 other juniors of mine!

As there were many things going on such as talks, sharing sessions and official & community villages, I mainly focused on the Maritime Hacking Village (super cool and eye opening village), as well as the C517 Village as they piqued my interest (as well as their goodies). For the Maritime Hacking Village's CTF, my team (3 juniors and I) managed to place 4th overall, barely missing on the top 2 prizes that was prepared. But for the C517 Village, I was lucky enough to complete all 6 of their levels right before the final day of the conference, leading me to obtain the village's 'Black' Badge! 

I have prepared writeups for both said villages for the challenges that I have solved. Enjoy the read! <3
