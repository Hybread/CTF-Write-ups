## Introduction
IBOH is a flagship CTF hosted annually by my university's (APU), Forensics and Cybersecurity Research Centre Student Section Club (FSEC-SS), which I am apart of.
This event's Stage 1/Preliminary Round featured a Local and International Category.
We were given a challenge sheet to fill our names in, and as usual, I filled two challenge slots for Reverse Engineering. As this was one of the very first CTF events where I was contributing towards, I took a lot of 
inspiration from HackTheBox' Cyber Apocalpyse 2025 challenges. The two challenges I created were `Celestial Gatekeeper`, a reference to HTB's `Gateway` challenge, and `BOH Ransom`, also a reference to HTB's `Heart Protector`.

To my surprise, in the 6 hours of competition, only `Celestial Gatekeeper` was solved, and it was solved by only 1 team out of both Local and International categories! To my honor, it was solved by players from 
[NUS GreyHats](https://nusgreyhats.org) from Singapore <3

Anyways, I hope you enjoy the read!
