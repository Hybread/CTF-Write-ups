# What is CYNX?

[CYNX](https://www.linkedin.com/company/cynx/), short for CyberNexus, is my very own beloved startup/hobby project! It's a mini community where we host cybersecurity workshops and CTF Competitions from time to time.
Our mission is to create a vibrant community mainly for up and coming cybersecurity students to learn, compete, and grow.
Obviously, I wish to try my best and expand this community as far as I can, but for the most part, this is just a test on myself on how much I'm able to balance my studies whilst managing a community!
And as long as I am able to provide opportunities for participants and my own members to grow, I'll be the happiest man alive XD
